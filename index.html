Community Gardening

There are many community garden projects around Edinburgh and the Lothians, as shown on the map below, just click on one of the icons to find out more! ELGT does not run these projects, but has been involved in quite a few of them, either by helping to set them up or supporting their ongoing activities. Other projects have been developed by grassroots groups, by community organisations, by NHS Lothian, or by other charities.

ELGT's Community Gardening Development Officer works to support new and existing community gardening initiatives around Edinburgh, particularly projects which will deliver health and wellbeing benefits to participants.

If you would like to find out more about community gardening initiatives in your area, or are looking for support and advice in setting up your own garden, please contact Rob on 0131 445 4025 or email rob@elgt.org.uk.

*New projects looking for volunteers - click on the carrots!*

Several new projects have started up in the last few months. Some are at a very early stage, having only just acquired a piece of land or only recently decided to start digging. Others have got a bit of momentum going now but are still keen to have more help.

All of these projects would welcome in eager volunteers, with or without gardening skills or knowledge. You can find these projects on the map below linked by the carrot icons.

ELREC Grow Your Own in Niddrie

Edinburgh and Lothians Regional Equality Council
has started a food growing group for people
from ethnic minorities in Niddrie, on a piece of land
offered by the Hunters Hall Co-operative.
This is an opportunity for people to have a space
to grow food in a nice environment, where you can
bring your families, meet with other people and
learn how to grow your own. Spaces are limited.
Sessions run every Saturday from the end of March
until October and last one and a half to two hours.
Last year we had a wonderful group and grew
many vegetables including carrots, radishes, kale, broccoli, etc.

LOCATION: Hunters Hall Housing Co-op, EH16 4TR
CONTACT: Parveen at PIshaq@elrec.org.uk or 0131 556 0441

ELREC Greenkeepers at the Botanic Gardens

Edinburgh and Lothians Regional Equality Council
